Frequently Asked Question

How does it work?

DevsZero analyses your endpoint and suggests a relevant component for your adoption. We'll then generate a full code in VueJS of the component.

Do you support any frontend frameworks?

Yes. We'll ship with VueJS. Generated components will be bundled together to a SPA. We look forward to support ReactJS and Angular in the near future.

Is DevsZero right for me?

If your requirement is to build a normal control dashboard, data view with CRUD, and few analytics, then is a solution for you.

How is licensing and pricing structured?

Our solution will be available on freemium basis. Early adopters are eligible for discounts when we finally launch.

Can I customize the theme colors?

You'll be able to customize the widgets to support your brand look and feel.

Widgets supported?

We plan to support over 100 components and this includes auth pages, charts, grids, CRUD etc.
